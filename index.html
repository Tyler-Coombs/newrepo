THE ANATOMY OF A BURGER

The traditonal form typically includes:

  • Warm/Toasted Bun
  • Dollop(s) of sauce of your choosing (ex. Ketchup, Mustard, Mayo, etc.)
  • A leafy green (ex. Crisp Lettuce)
  • Pickles
  • Onion
  • Juicy Tomato
  • Bacon (a must IMO)
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Beef Patty
  • Bottom Bun

Of course, this is just a template. There are no rules when it comes to burgers - a great example is the addition of a fried egg like in the image above.
You can be as brave or as modest with toppings as you like! Patties can be made from any meat or even plant-based substitutes.
